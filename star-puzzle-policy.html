Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

1. Introduction

Welcome to Star Puzzle Mania! We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our users. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our game.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information automatically collected:

  • Game progress data (scores, levels achieved)
  • Gameplay statistics (play time, number of sessions)
  • Basic device information (device type, operating system)
  • Crash data and errors to improve the game

2.2 Information we do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, address, phone number)
  • Contact data or address book
  • Financial information
  • Precise location data

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Improve your gaming experience
  • Develop and update new features
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Provide technical support when needed

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption during transmission
  • Secure storage on protected servers
  • Strict access controls
  • Regular security updates

5. Information Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law enforcement or legal authorities
  • To protect the rights and safety of other users
  • With your explicit consent

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your game data
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • File complaints about data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Star Puzzle Mania is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The game may use local storage technologies to:

  • Remember your game settings
  • Save game progress
  • Improve game performance

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated through:

  • In-game notifications
  • Email notifications (if available)
  • Official website
