Privacy Policy

Multiple QR Scanner

1
Introduction

Welcome to Multiple QR Scanner! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while using our QR code scanning and generating application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

By using Multiple QR Scanner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2
Information We Collect

Camera Access: Our app requires camera permission to scan QR codes and barcodes. We do not store or transmit any images captured by your camera.

QR Code Data: When you scan QR codes, the decoded information is processed locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.

Generated QR Codes: QR codes you create using our app are generated locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them.

Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

• Provide core functionality: Enable QR code scanning and generation features

• Improve user experience: Optimize app performance and add new features

• Ensure security: Protect against misuse and maintain app integrity

• Provide support: Help resolve technical issues when contacted

5
Data Storage & Security

Local Processing: All QR code scanning and generation is performed locally on your device. Your scanned data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

No Cloud Storage: We do not store your QR code data, scan history, or personal information on remote servers.

Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.
