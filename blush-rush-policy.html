Privacy Policy

for Blush Rush

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Blush Rush.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Blush Rush may collect the following information:

  • Game progress and achievements
  • High scores and level completion data
  • In-app purchase information (if applicable)
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve game performance

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • Game usage statistics and analytics
  • IP address and general location information
  • Advertising identifiers for personalized ads

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze game usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair play

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate the game (analytics, advertising, cloud storage)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Third-Party Services

Blush Rush may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Games Services: For achievements and leaderboards
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Firebase Analytics: For game analytics and crash reporting
  • Unity Analytics: For game performance monitoring

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure servers and databases

6. Children's Privacy

Blush Rush is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the game service and for legitimate business purposes. Game progress data is typically retained for the lifetime of your account or until you request deletion.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

9. Advertising

Blush Rush may display advertisements. We use advertising identifiers to show relevant ads. You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information during such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the game or on our website. Your continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
