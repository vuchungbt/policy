1. Introduction

Welcome to Anti-Theft Phone Alarm ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not download, register with, or use this application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Device Information

To provide our anti-theft services, we may collect the following device information:

  • Device model, manufacturer, and operating system version
  • Device sensors data (accelerometer, gyroscope) for motion detection
  • Audio access for alarm sounds
  • Device settings and preferences

2.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our app, including:

  • App features used and frequency of use
  • Alarm activation patterns
  • Settings configurations
  • App performance and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To detect unauthorized movement and trigger appropriate alarms
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Technical Support: To provide customer support and troubleshoot issues
  • Security: To prevent fraud and ensure app security

4. Permissions Required

Our app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Microphone: To play alarm sounds at maximum volume
  • Device Motion: To detect phone movement for theft detection
  • Storage: To save your preferences and settings
  • Wake Lock: To keep the app active when monitoring for theft

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored locally on your device whenever possible
  • We use industry-standard encryption for any data transmission
  • Regular security audits and updates to protect against vulnerabilities
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
Important: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any information.

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand app usage. These services may collect information sent by your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
