Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Love Day Counter collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our relationship tracking application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Love Day Counter, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Relationship Data: Anniversary dates, milestone information, and relationship goals you choose to track
  • User Preferences: App themes, notification settings, and customization choices
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about how you interact with app features

Information We Don't Collect

We respect your privacy and do not collect:

  • Personal photos or images
  • Contact information from your device
  • Location data
  • Messages or communication content
  • Social media account information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To calculate and display your relationship milestones and day counts
  • Personalization: To provide customized themes and features based on your preferences
  • Notifications: To send reminders about important dates and milestones (if enabled)
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your relationship data is primarily stored locally on your device to ensure privacy and quick access. This includes:

  • Anniversary and milestone dates
  • Personal goals and achievements
  • App preferences and settings

Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup:

  • Data is encrypted before transmission
  • We use industry-standard security protocols
  • You can disable cloud backup at any time
  • Backup data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by our team
  • Secure servers with industry-standard protection

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited, non-personal information only in these circumstances:

  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:

  • Calendar applications (for milestone reminders)
  • Notification services (for anniversary alerts)
  • Analytics tools (for app improvement)

These integrations are optional and can be disabled in app settings.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app
  • Edit: Modify or update your relationship information
  • Delete: Remove specific data or all data from the app
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics, notifications, or cloud backup

How to Exercise Your Rights

To manage your data or exercise your rights:

  • Use the "Privacy Settings" section within the app
  • Contact our support team using the information below
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

6. Children's Privacy

Love Day Counter is intended for users who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

Love Day Counter is available worldwide. If you are using our app from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your country.

We ensure that any international data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and regulations.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make changes:

  • We will notify you through the app or by email
  • The updated policy will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Significant changes will require your explicit consent

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Active Users: Data is retained while you use the app
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
