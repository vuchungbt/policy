Game Rules & Guidelines

How to Play

  • Control your spaceship to avoid and destroy enemy UFOs
  • Collect coins and power-ups for higher scores
  • Survive as long as possible to beat your high score
  • Game gets progressively more challenging

Scoring System

  • Destroy UFOs to earn points
  • Collect coins for bonus points
  • Survival time contributes to final score
  • High scores are saved locally on your device

Fair Play Policy

We encourage fair and honest gameplay. Any attempt to manipulate scores or use unauthorized tools may result in score resets or game access restrictions.

Remember: UFO Blaster is designed to be fun and challenging. Play responsibly and take breaks when needed!
