Core Principle: Your personal data stays personal. We use your information solely to provide and improve the Dailyfy experience.

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate and display your productivity statistics and summaries

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your personal data (diary entries, financial records, time logs) is stored locally on your device. This means your private information remains under your direct control.

Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud synchronization, your encrypted data may be stored on secure cloud servers to allow access across your devices. You can disable this feature at any time in the app settings.

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • No unauthorized access to your personal content
