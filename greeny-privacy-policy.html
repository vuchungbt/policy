Greeny Game

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Greeny.

1. Information We Collect

When you play Greeny, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your level progress, high scores, achievements, and in-game statistics
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game, session duration, and feature usage
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases for power-ups and game enhancements
  • Technical Data: IP address, crash reports, and performance metrics to improve game stability

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Greeny gaming experience
  • Save your game progress and settings across devices
  • Process in-app purchases and unlock premium features
  • Analyze game performance and fix technical issues
  • Improve gameplay mechanics and add new features
  • Send important game updates and notifications (if you opt-in)

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Analytics Services: Anonymous usage data to understand player behavior and improve the game
  • Cloud Storage Providers: To securely backup your game progress
  • Payment Processors: To handle in-app purchases securely
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure servers and data centers with robust protection measures

5. Children's Privacy

Greeny is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove such information.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Disable analytics and advertising tracking (where available)

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide game services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Game progress data is kept while your account is active. If you delete the game or request account deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify you of any significant changes through in-game notifications or other appropriate means. Continued use of Greeny after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and that your information receives adequate protection.
