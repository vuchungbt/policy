Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Heaven Blaster! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress Data: Your level progression, scores, and achievements
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game to improve gameplay experience
  • Crash Reports: Technical information to help us fix bugs and improve stability

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game service
  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Analyze game usage patterns to enhance gameplay
  • Provide customer support when needed

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your game data is stored securely and we regularly review our security practices.

5. Third-Party Services

Our game may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other features. These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy.

6. Children's Privacy

Heaven Blaster is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as necessary to provide the service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date below. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. International Users

If you are playing Heaven Blaster from outside our primary jurisdiction, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where our servers are located.
