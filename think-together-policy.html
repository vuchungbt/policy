Effective Date: Sep 04, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Think Together.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Game Data

We collect and store the following information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game progress and level completion
  • High scores and best completion times
  • In-game currency (coins and gems)
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Achievement and milestone data

1.2 Device Information

We may collect basic device information including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version information
  • Language settings
  • General location (country/region only)

1.3 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized gameplay statistics to improve the game experience:

  • Level completion rates
  • Time spent in game
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Technical performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Game Functionality: To save your progress, maintain leaderboards, and provide personalized gaming experience
  • Game Improvement: To analyze gameplay patterns and improve game balance and features
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
  • Security: To detect and prevent cheating or fraudulent activities

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help us operate the game (e.g., cloud storage, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure cloud storage with reputable providers

5. Children's Privacy

Think Together is suitable for all ages. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No advertising or marketing communications are sent to players
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's information

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored game data
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your game progress data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection in game settings

7. Cookies and Tracking

Think Together may use:

  • Local storage to save game progress and settings
  • Analytics cookies to understand game usage (can be disabled)
  • Performance cookies to optimize game loading and functionality

8. Third-Party Services

Our game may integrate with third-party services for:

  • Cloud save functionality
  • Anonymous usage analytics
  • Crash reporting and performance monitoring

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in different countries. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard contractual clauses with service providers
  • Compliance with applicable data protection laws
  • Regular privacy impact assessments

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with an updated effective date
  • Notified through in-game announcements for significant changes
  • Sent via email if we have your contact information
