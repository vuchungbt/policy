About Fruit Horizontal Sort Puzzle

Fruit Slide Puzzle is an engaging match-three puzzle game where players arrange fruits in horizontal rows to score points. Slide fruits, create matches, and unlock exciting levels in this colorful and addictive gaming experience!

Terms of Service

1. Game Rules

Fruit Slide Puzzle is a sliding puzzle game where the objective is to arrange fruits in horizontal rows to earn points. The game mechanics include:

  • Sliding fruits horizontally or vertically on the game board
  • Creating matches of three or more identical fruits in a horizontal row
  • Earning points for each match, with bonus points for longer matches
  • Completing level objectives to progress through the game
