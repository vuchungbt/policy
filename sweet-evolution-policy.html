Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Sweet Evolution! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while playing our delicious puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Progress Data: Your high scores, level progression, achievements, and in-game preferences
  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we may collect your username and email address
  • Purchase Information: Records of in-app purchases and transactions

Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the game, session duration, features used
  • Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and technical diagnostics
  • Advertising Data: Information related to ads served within the game

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the Sweet Evolution gaming experience
  • Save your game progress and preferences across devices
  • Improve game performance and fix technical issues
  • Personalize your gaming experience and provide relevant content
  • Process in-app purchases and transactions
  • Send important game updates and notifications
  • Analyze game usage to enhance features and gameplay
  • Serve relevant advertisements and promotional content

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us operate the game (analytics, cloud storage, payment processing)
  • Advertising Partners: Networks that serve ads within our game
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
Important: We never sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Sweet Evolution may integrate with third-party services including:

  • Google Play Games / Game Center for achievements and leaderboards
  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks (AdMob, Unity Ads)
  • Social media platforms for sharing features
  • Cloud save services for cross-device synchronization

Children's Privacy

Sweet Evolution is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for all ages. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure servers and data centers

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out: Disable certain data collection features in game settings
  • Advertising: Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Game progress data is typically retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

International Data Transfers

Sweet Evolution is available globally, and your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes through in-app notifications or other appropriate means. Your continued use of Sweet Evolution after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
