Fruit Slash Catch

Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Fruit Slash Catch! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our puzzle mining game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

2. Information We Collect

Game Data

  • Game progress and achievements
  • High scores and best completion times
  • In-game currency (coins) and purchases
  • Level completion statistics
  • Game settings and preferences

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and performance data
  • Crash reports and error logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress and maintain your profile
  • Provide personalized gaming experience
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Analyze gameplay patterns to enhance game design
  • Provide customer support when needed

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Most of your game data is stored locally on your device, including progress, scores, and settings.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our game may integrate with third-party services for:

  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Advertising (if applicable)
  • Social features and leaderboards

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Children's Privacy

Fruit Slash Catch is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as you have the game installed and actively use it. You can delete your data by uninstalling the game or using the data deletion options in the game settings.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection where possible
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the game and updating the "Last Updated" date below.

10. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside our primary jurisdiction, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.
