Privacy Policy

Flashlight & Compass App

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Flashlight & Compass application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device Information

Our app may collect the following device information to provide core functionality:

  • Camera Access: Required for flashlight functionality to control the device's LED flash
  • Sensor Data: Compass and magnetometer data for compass functionality
  • Device Orientation: To properly display compass directions
  • Battery Information: To optimize flashlight usage and prevent battery drain

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance:

  • App usage frequency and duration
  • Feature usage patterns (flashlight vs. compass)
  • Device performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs

1.3 Rewards System Data

For our points and achievements system, we store:

  • Usage milestones and achievements
  • Points earned through app usage
  • User preferences for notifications

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide flashlight and compass features
  • Performance Optimization: To improve app speed and reliability
  • User Experience: To enhance features and user interface
  • Rewards System: To track achievements and provide incentives
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel have access to user data
  • Regular Updates: We regularly update security measures and protocols

4. Permissions Required

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To access the device's LED flash for flashlight functionality
  • Sensors: To access compass and magnetometer for direction features
  • Vibration: For haptic feedback and notifications
  • Wake Lock: To keep the screen on during flashlight use

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services for enhanced functionality:

  • Analytics Services: For app performance monitoring (anonymized data only)
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues
  • Ad Networks: If applicable, for non-intrusive advertising

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Usage Data: Anonymized usage data may be retained for up to 2 years
  • Achievement Data: Stored locally on your device
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 1 year for debugging purposes
  • Device Data: Not permanently stored, used only during app sessions

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable data collection in app settings
  • Permissions: Revoke app permissions through device settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the app and all associated data

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify users through the app or other means
  • The updated policy will be posted in the app
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the new policy
  • Material changes will require explicit user consent

10. International Users

Our app is available globally. If you are using our app from outside your country of residence, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries with different privacy laws.
