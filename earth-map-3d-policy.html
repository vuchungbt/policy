üìçLocation Data Policy

How We Use Location Data

Feature Enhancement Only: We use your location data solely to enhance app functionality, including:

  • Showing nearby cameras and points of interest
  • Providing location-relevant content and recommendations
  • Enabling location-based features like compass and local time display
  • Improving map accuracy and user experience

What We DON'T Do

We Do NOT Collect or Store Your Location Data:

  • Your location information is processed locally on your device only
  • We do not transmit your location data to our servers
  • We do not store your location history
  • We do not share your location with third parties
  • We do not track your movements or create location profiles

Your Control: You can disable location services for Earth Map 3D at any time through your device settings. The app will continue to function, though some location-based features may be limited.
