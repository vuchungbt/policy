Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you play Wood Pop.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you use Wood Pop, we automatically collect the following technical information to ensure the game functions properly:

  • Device ID: A unique identifier for your device to maintain game settings and preferences
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and hardware specifications
  • Game Progress: Your scores, levels completed, and achievements
  • Tracking Logs: Information about how you interact with the game features
  • Error Logs: Technical errors and crashes that occur during gameplay
  • Performance Data: Device performance metrics such as frame rates, memory usage, and loading times
  • IP Address: General location information (country/region level only)

Optional Information

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information. The game can be played anonymously. Any additional information you choose to share is entirely optional.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect serves specific purposes to improve your gaming experience:

  • Deliver and maintain core game functionality
  • Optimize game performance based on device capabilities
  • Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance game features
  • Provide technical support when requested
  • Ensure fair gameplay and prevent cheating
  • Improve overall user experience based on aggregated data

We are committed to transparent and responsible data practices:

  • Strict Policy Compliance: All data collection and usage strictly adheres to this privacy policy. We will never use your data in ways not disclosed here.
  • Automatic Data Deletion: All collected data (including device IDs, tracking logs, error logs, and performance data) is automatically deleted from our servers after 90 days. This ensures your information is not stored indefinitely.
  • No Selling of Data: We never sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Minimal Collection: We only collect data that is essential for game functionality and improvement.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and handle your information with care. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share your information
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate the game (analytics, cloud storage) under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to comply with legal processes
  • Safety and Security: To protect our rights, users' safety, or prevent fraud

Important: Any shared data is anonymized whenever possible and is subject to the same 90-day deletion policy.

4. Third-Party Services

Wood Pop may integrate with the following types of third-party services:

  • Analytics Services: To understand player behavior and improve the game (e.g., game session duration, feature usage)
  • Cloud Storage: To optionally back up your game progress across devices
  • Crash Reporting Tools: To identify and fix technical issues quickly

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We carefully select partners who maintain high privacy standards, but we encourage you to review their policies independently.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Access controls limiting who can view collected data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure servers with firewall protection

While we strive to protect your information, no internet transmission method is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to improve our protections.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We believe in minimal data retention:

  • Automatic Deletion: All server-side data is automatically deleted after 90 days
  • Local Data: Game progress stored on your device remains until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Manual Deletion: You can request immediate deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Account Deletion: If you choose to create an optional account, you can delete it anytime, removing all associated data

7. Children's Privacy

Wood Pop is designed for players of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • The game does not require personal information to play
  • We do not use targeted advertising based on children's data
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete any information we may have about their child

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request immediate deletion of your data (before the 90-day automatic deletion)
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and tracking in your device settings
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent at any time

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that:

  • All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data
  • The same 90-day deletion policy applies regardless of data location

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be announced within the game
  • You may be asked to review and accept updated terms
  • Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance
