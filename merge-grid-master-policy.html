Merge Grid Master

Privacy Policy

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play Merge Grid Master.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Merge Grid Master is designed to be enjoyed without requiring personal information. We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photos, contacts, or other personal data
  • Social media profiles or accounts

1.2 Game Data

We may collect non-personal game-related information to improve your experience:

  • Game progress and level completion
  • High scores and achievements
  • In-game purchases (if applicable)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Game crash reports and performance data

2. How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the game service
  • Saving your game progress locally on your device
  • Improving game performance and fixing bugs
  • Developing new features and content
  • Ensuring fair play and preventing cheating

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Advertisement Networks

Merge Grid Master may display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may collect certain information to show relevant ads:

  • Device advertising ID
  • General location (country/region)
  • App usage data

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

3.2 Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how players interact with our game. This helps us improve gameplay and user experience. All data is anonymized and aggregated.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Game data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Any transmitted data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We regularly update our security practices
  • Access to any collected data is strictly limited

5. Children's Privacy

Merge Grid Master is suitable for players of all ages. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No chat features or social interactions with strangers
  • Parental controls are available for in-app purchases
  • Content is appropriate for all age groups

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Delete the app to remove locally stored game data
  • Opt-out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Contact us to request information about data practices
  • Disable analytics through device privacy settings

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary:

  • Game progress is stored locally until you uninstall the app
  • Analytics data is retained for up to 24 months
  • Crash reports are deleted after resolution

8. International Data Transfers

If any data is transmitted outside your country, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy in accordance with applicable laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will post the updated policy in the app
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we may provide additional notice
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes
